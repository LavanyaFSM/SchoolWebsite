About First Step Montessori

Building Foundations for Lifelong Learning

At First Step Montessori, we believe that every child is unique and possesses an innate desire to learn. Our carefully prepared environment nurtures independence, creativity, and a love for discovery.

Following the Montessori philosophy, we provide hands-on learning experiences that allow children to explore at their own pace, developing confidence and critical thinking skills that will serve them throughout their lives.

Learning Pathways

Comprehensive Development Through Montessori Methods

Practical Life

Children develop independence and fine motor skills through everyday activities like pouring, sorting, and caring for their environment. These foundational exercises build concentration and coordination.

Sensorial Exploration

Specialized Montessori materials help children refine their senses and develop cognitive skills. Through hands-on exploration, they learn to classify, compare, and understand the world around them.

Language & Literacy

Our phonetic approach to reading and writing helps children develop strong language skills. Through storytelling, conversation, and hands-on materials, literacy becomes a joyful journey.

Signature Programs

Experiential Learning Journeys

Nature Explorers

Outdoor learning experiences that connect children with nature, fostering environmental awareness and scientific curiosity through garden activities and nature walks.

Creative Arts Studio

Music, art, and movement programs that encourage self-expression and creativity while developing fine motor skills and artistic appreciation.

Math & Science Discovery

Hands-on mathematical and scientific exploration using Montessori materials that make abstract concepts concrete and engaging for young learners.

Cultural Celebrations

Exploring diverse cultures, traditions, and languages to build global awareness and appreciation for the rich tapestry of human experience.

Life Skills Workshop

Practical activities that build independence, from self-care routines to cooking and gardening, preparing children for real-world responsibilities.

Social & Emotional Learning

Building emotional intelligence, conflict resolution skills, and positive relationships through guided activities and peaceful communication practices.

Meet Our Director

Leading with Vision and Passion

Lavanya Sanjay

Founder & Director

With over 12 years of experience in early childhood education and a deep commitment to the Montessori philosophy, Lavanya Sanjay founded First Step Montessori with a vision to create a nurturing environment where every child can discover their unique potential. Her passion for child development and innovative teaching methods has shaped our school into a beacon of excellence in Montessori education.

Lavanya Sanjay holds a Ph.D. in Early Childhood Education and is a certified Montessori trainer. She believes that education should be a joyful journey of discovery, where children learn to think independently, solve problems creatively, and develop a lifelong love for learning.
